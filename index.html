Why should we help?

Aging is a natural part of our lives. Though it is something celebrated, it still has its downsides. Aging may cause reduced physical mobility, increased risk of sickness, and more that affect the brain and body. In cases where the family cannot support individuals in this stage of life, organizations like Good Samaritan step in to help them. Our program would like to help organizations like this as we believe in their mission.

How You Can Help

Visit and Spend Time with Them

The elderly often experience loneliness. Therefore, it is important to spend time with them like playing board games.

Assist with Donations

A helping hand is always appreciated. We can always give donations to these organizations. Financial aid or groceries would be greatly appreciated.

Join the Outreach Program

Partake with us on January 22, 2025 on our outreach program. Any help will go a long way!
